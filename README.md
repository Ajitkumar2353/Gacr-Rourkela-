# Gacr-Rourkela-
Government Autonomous College, Rourkela (GACR) is a premier government institution in Odisha, established in 1961. Affiliated with Sambalpur University and granted autonomous status in 2002, it offers UG, PG, and self-financing programs in Arts, Science, Commerce, and Computer Science. The college is NAAC accredited and known for quality education.
