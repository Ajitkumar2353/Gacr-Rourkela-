About the College

Government Autonomous College, Rourkela (GACR), formerly known as Rourkela Science College, was established in 1961. The college became a Government College in 1963 and was granted Autonomous Status in 2002. It is affiliated with Sambalpur University and offers quality higher education in Arts, Science, Commerce and Computer Science.
